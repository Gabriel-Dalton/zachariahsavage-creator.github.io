Who Am I?

Zach Savage is a Toronto-based Photographer, specializing in concert and event photography. Shooting since 2022, Zach's eye is tuned to follow the energy of each performance. His photographic styling aims to serve the unique personality and aesthetics of every artist and event he shoots.
